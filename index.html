Seeing life as an opportunity to explore, experience and discover what it means to be fully alive.

The body and mind are part of that journey;
yoga is the practice of observing their movements with awareness,
rather than becoming identified with them.

Yoga is happening in every moment of life. The practice is learning to recognize it.
