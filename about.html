Hi, I am Daphne


The WHY and WHAT bring me to the practice?

Slowly, I began to realize that yoga is not something I do—it is a way of relating to life.
The practice is not confined to the mat. It unfolds in every moment of life: in how we move, think, feel, relate, respond, and simply be.
We practice acceptance, humility, and kindness as a way of meeting the human world—with awareness, compassion, and understanding. We are given the space to unlearn, relearn, and rediscover ourselves, slowly coming home to ourselves.
